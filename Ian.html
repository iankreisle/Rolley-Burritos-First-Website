Welcome to Ian's Page!


My name is Ian. I am a GIMM student who likes games and coding. The red fox is my favorite animal. My favorite game is Rimworld. The game is a colony managment simulation set in the far edges of habited space. You control a group of colonists that have crash landed on a distant rimworld and must survive long enough to escape the planet... or establish yourself as a force to be feared.

The game is very open ended in how you play. You can set up your colony however you wish, tackle problems in many ways, progress in research in near any order you want, and create stories based on the events that happen in game. Rimworld is often described as a story generator, and I completly agree with this assessment.
